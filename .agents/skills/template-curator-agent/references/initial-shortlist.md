5. **Restoran or Restaurant Website** as restaurant visual inspiration only until licensing is clarified.

## Seraphim Polish Required

- Replace generic/filler copy with cleaned business data.
- Upgrade SEO head, schema, FAQ, footer, sticky nav, and CTA flow.
- Remove fake testimonials, fake counts, fake doctors, fake prices, and unsupported claims.
- Replace stock or template imagery with niche-matched remote imagery or supplied business photos.
- Translate useful patterns into Seraphim single-file static HTML/CSS/JS.
- Add mobile QA, overflow checks, and scroll stability checks before a pack can ship.
