# Seraphim brand spec

## Verified asset

- Logo: `public/seraphim-logo.png`
- Source: supplied by the product owner on 2026-07-11.
- Usage: use the supplied wing mark as an image; do not redraw it with CSS or SVG.

## Product UI direction

- Primary color: blue teal, anchored at `#0b94b2`.
- Deep surface: `#063b4c` to `#075f77` for high-emphasis workflow moments.
- Supporting accent: cyan, anchored at `#35c4df`.
- Light tint: `#ecfbff` for active states and onboarding guidance.
- Neutral canvas: `#f6fcfe` with white panels.
- Typography: existing Inter/Avenir Next system is retained for continuity and readability.
- Shape language: rounded 12–32px corners, thin cool-gray borders, restrained shadows.

The logo is intentionally placed on a white tile so its supplied white background remains legible in both light navigation and the dark dashboard hero.
